EdemaSense: Early Congestion Detection

A research-facing prototype built around a narrower clinical question than a generic heart-failure score: can a technology-derived extracellular fluid signal identify worsening congestion before obvious decompensation, hospitalization, or an ED visit?

Concept demo only. Not a validated model, not a medical device, and not for patient care.

    • Track the fluid signal next to short-term weight change.

    • Show change over time instead of only showing a one-time score.
